The Process

Your Complete 5-Step
Surrogacy Roadmap in Nigeria

Everything you need to know from legal requirements to clinic selection — broken into clear, manageable stages.

Step 01
Create Your Profile

Sign up and tell us about your journey — whether you're an intended parent or a surrogate. Your profile is private and secure.

Step 02
Explore the Resource Hub

Access 63 expert articles covering legal rights, IVF procedures, cost breakdowns, emotional wellness, and real success stories from Nigerian families.

Step 03
Connect with Legal Experts

Browse verified legal specialists in surrogacy law, parental rights, adoption, medical agreements, and contract law — all Nigeria-based and vetted.

Step 04
Find Your Clinic

Browse trusted, verified fertility clinics across Nigeria — from Lagos to Port Harcourt — with real ratings, locations, and consultation booking.

Step 05
Book Your Consultation

Choose your specialist or clinic, pick a preferred date and time slot, select WhatsApp or phone as your contact method, and request your consultation securely.
